Software Development Life Cycle

TOur department supports waterfall, agile, and hybrid Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) approaches, which are used as the process guidance for designing, developing, configuring, testing, delivering, and supporting quality software solutions. Our department's SDLC aims to guide cross-functional teams through all of the necessary steps required to produce solutions that meet or exceed client requirements. Our SDLC provides the framework that defines in detail all of the tasks performed at each step in the software development process including all of the necessary supporting material. Below is a high-level diagram describing the composition of our SDLC. A detailed version can be viewed and is available by clicking on the "View Full Diagram" link the bottom of the page.

Continuous Integration / Continuous Delivery Workflow

We have developed a departmental Continuous Integration / Continuous Delivery (CI/CD) workflow that is comprised of the industry development best practices that are ideal for cross-functional team autonomy, efficiency, and conveys the disciplines needed to deliver secure high quality software. A detailed version of the CI/CD diagram is available by clicking on the "View Full Diagram" link the bottom of the page.
